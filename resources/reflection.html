Reflection



Creating the site was a learning process with mistakes included which made me find skills to solve them. I had some problems with the responsive design and how images fit into each container. I also had to take into account extra elements crucial for interactivity and efficiency within the web like arrows and scrolls. Taking the content into account I had to establish different sections and containers to include the information and photos but it was challenging to make it look balanced and organised. I also wanted to add tables and more interactive elements like emojis across the page to make it funner. Furthermore, for the reflection section I decided to make a dropdown so that it looked cleaner visually. For the legend I also struggled to make it disappear when scrolling down but with lots of trials I achieved the desired result.

This practice made me realise the importance of trial and error and how content conditions aesthetics in web design. I sometimes struggle to organised all the data so it was a struggle to keep everything neat and clear but I am content and proud with the final result.
